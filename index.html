About

Order in complex environments

At Deloitte, I steer cyber design for Oracle and cloud ERP: access models, SoD controls, and governance that hold up when stakeholders disagree. The aim: security that ships and stays measurable.

Before that at ASML’s Chief Data Office, I got engineers and finance to trust the same data without needing heroes in the loop. Data governance and quality, but practical.

At Capgemini, I pushed GenAI and automation into real use—hours saved, risk reduced, not just clever demos.

Through-line: I like messy, high-impact systems. I map the politics, design the guardrails, and leave teams with controls they can actually operate.
