About

Structured thinking for complex environments

At Deloitte, I work on cyber and Oracle/cloud ERP programs – designing access models, SoD controls and governance that survive real-world politics. The brief is simple: make security usable and measurable.

Before consulting, I was in ASML's Chief Data Office, fixing data governance and quality so engineers and finance could trust the same numbers without heroic workarounds.

Earlier at Capgemini, I pushed GenAI and automation into use cases that saved time and reduced risk instead of just making nice demos.

The through-line: I like untangling complex, political environments and leaving behind structured, auditable systems people can actually operate.
